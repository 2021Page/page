FAQ

  • Q. How long does delivery take?

    * It takes about 1~3 days on average from the date of shipment. (Excluding weekends/holidays)
    * When the product is released, the invoice number will be sent via AlimTalk or SMS.
    * All delivery may be delayed due to local courier circumstances and natural disasters.
    * Separate guidance will be provided in case of non-delivery due to congestion, delay in delivery, or out of stock.

  • Q. What are the hours of use of the customer center?

    [Luminous Customer Center Operation Guide]
    : 365 days 09:00~18:00 (Lunch time 11:50~13:00)

  • Q. What should I do if I want to exchange/return/refund?

    [When exchange/return is applicable]
    * Exchanges and returns because of a simple change of mind are possible within 7 days, including weekends and holidays, after receiving the product. (Shipping cost must be borne by the customer)
    * Exchanges and returns because of defective products are possible within 3 months of the date of supply. (Due to period)
    [When exchange/return is not applicable]
    * Exchanges or returns are not possible if the product value is significantly reduced because of consumer's reputation or use.
    * Defective within 10 days of delivery (based on calendar days): Free exchange

  • Q. I want to make an inquiry, but I can't get through to the customer service center.

    On Mondays, there may be a lot of inquiries, so the phone connection may not be smooth.
    If you ask me again soon, I will try to answer quickly.

  • Q. How much is the shipping fee?

    Standard shipping is $3, and free shipping on orders over $30.
    (However, Jeju and special island and mountainous areas will incur an additional fare of $4~$6)

  • Q. Is there any benefit for registering as a new membership?

    If you join Ruminous, you will receive a coupon worth 5000 won.
    It can only be used in the Ruminous online shopping mall.[Can't Use off-line store]
    *Term of Use: Within 7 days of membership
    * Available for purchases over KRW 10,000

  • Q. How can I find the perfect size for my nails?

    Luminous gel consists of 15 types of size & 30 pcs, and can be used in various nail sizes.
    If you don't have the right size, you can attach the Luminous gel more perfectly if you stretch it slightly to fit your nail size.

  • Q. How do I use a gel lamp?

    Ohorazelamp is a USB power supply product, not a rechargeable product.
    After connecting the USB line to the adapter or secondary battery, press the button next to the main body to operate, and it will automatically turn off after about 45 seconds of operation.
